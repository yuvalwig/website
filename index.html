Yuval Wigderson

I am a Junior Fellow at ETH-ITS, where I am affiliated with the Mathematics Department of ETH Zürich and hosted by Benny Sudakov. My research interests include extremal and probabilistic combinatorics, Ramsey theory, and related topics.

I graduated from Stanford, where I did my PhD with Jacob Fox. I was subsequently a postdoctoral fellow at Tel Aviv University, hosted by Michael Krivelevich, Wojciech Samotij, and Asaf Shapira
